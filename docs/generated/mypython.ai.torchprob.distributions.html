class mypython.ai.torchprob.distributions.gmm.GMM(pi: Union[None, int, float, Tensor] = None, mu: Union[None, int, float, Tensor] = None, sigma: Union[None, int, float, Tensor] = None)

Bases: Distribution

Gaussian Mixture Model

\[\begin{array}{ll} p(\boldsymbol{x} \mid \boldsymbol{\Theta}) = \displaystyle \sum_{k=1}^K \pi_k \, \mathcal{N}(\boldsymbol{x} \mid \boldsymbol{\mu}_k, \boldsymbol{\Sigma}_k) \end{array}\]

“Normal (Gaussian) distribution” is not multivariate normal distribution.

Constraint:

\[\begin{array}{ll} \displaystyle \sum_{k=1}^K \pi_k = 1 \end{array}\]
Inputs: pi, mu, sigma

  • pi: tensor of shape \((N, K)\)

  • mu: tensor of shape \((N, K, D)\)

  • sigma: tensor of shape \((N, K, D)\)

where:

\[\begin{split}\begin{aligned} K ={} & \text{number of mixtures} \\ D ={} & \text{number of features} \\ \end{aligned}\end{split}\]
