class models.core.NewtonianVAE(*args, **kwargs)

Bases: NewtonianVAEBase

Computes ELBO based on formula (11).

Computes according to the following formula:

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{ll} \\ A = f(\x_{t-1}, \v_{t-1}, \u_{t-1}) \\ B = -\log f(\x_{t-1}, \v_{t-1}, \u_{t-1}) \\ C = \log f(\x_{t-1}, \v_{t-1}, \u_{t-1}) \\ \v_t = \v_{t-1} + \Delta t \cdot (A\x_{t-1} + B\v_{t-1} + C\u_{t-1}) \\ \x_{t} \sim p(\x_t \mid \x_{t-1}, \u_{t-1}; \v_{t}) \\ E = \displaystyle \sum_t \left( \log p(\I_t \mid \x_t) - \KL{q(\x_t \mid \I_t)}{p(\x_t \mid \x_{t-1}, \u_{t-1}; \v_{t})} \right) \end{array}\end{split}\]

where:

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{ll} \v_0 = \boldsymbol{0} \\ \x_0 \sim q(\x_0 \mid \I_0) \\ p(\x_t \mid \x_{t-1}, \u_{t-1}) = \mathcal{N}(\x_t \mid \x_{t-1} + \Delta t \cdot \v_t, \sigma^2) \\ \x_{t-2} \leftarrow \x_{t-1} \end{array}\end{split}\]

During evaluation:

\[\begin{array}{ll} \v_{t-1} = (\x_{t-1} - \x_{t-2}) / \Delta t \end{array}\]
Inputs: action, observation, dt

  • action: tensor of shape \((T, N, D)\)

  • observation: tensor of shape \((T, N, C, H, W)\)

  • dt: tensor of shape \((T)\)

where:

\[\begin{split}\begin{aligned} N ={} & \text{batch size} \\ T ={} & \text{sequence length} \\ D ={} & \mathrm{dim}(\u) \\ \end{aligned}\end{split}\]
References in paper:

  • During inference, vt is computed as vt = (xt − xt−1)/∆t, with xt ∼ q(xt|It) and xt−1 ∼ q(xt−1|It−1).

  • we added an additional regularization term to the latent space, KL(q(x|I)‖N (0, 1))

class models.core.NewtonianVAEV2(*args, **kwargs)

Bases: NewtonianVAEBase

Computes ELBO based on formula (11).

Computes according to the following formula:

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{ll} \\ \x_{t-1} \sim q(\x_{t-1} \mid \I_{t-1}) \\ \v_{t-1} = (\x_{t-1} - \x_{t-2}) / \Delta t \\ A = \mathrm{diag}(f(\x_{t-1}, \v_{t-1}, \u_{t-1})) \\ B = -\log \mathrm{diag}(f(\x_{t-1}, \v_{t-1}, \u_{t-1})) \\ C = \log \mathrm{diag}(f(\x_{t-1}, \v_{t-1}, \u_{t-1})) \\ \v_t = \v_{t-1} + \Delta t \cdot (A\x_{t-1} + B\v_{t-1} + C\u_{t-1}) \\ \x_{t} \sim p(\x_t \mid \x_{t-1}, \u_{t-1}; \v_{t}) \\ ELBO = \displaystyle \sum_t \left( \log p(\I_t \mid \x_t) - \KL{q(\x_t \mid \I_t)}{p(\x_t \mid \x_{t-1}, \u_{t-1}; \v_{t})} \right) \end{array}\end{split}\]

where:

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{ll} p(\x_t \mid \x_{t-1}, \u_{t-1}) = \mathcal{N}(\x_t \mid \x_{t-1} + \Delta t \cdot \v_t, \sigma^2) \\ \x_{t-2} \leftarrow \x_{t-1} \end{array}\end{split}\]

The initial values follow the formula below:

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{ll} \v_0 = \boldsymbol{0} \\ \x_0 \sim q(\x_0 \mid \I_0) \end{array}\end{split}\]

LOG_x is collected about \(\x_{t} \sim q(\x_t \mid \I_t)\).

  • action: tensor of shape \((T, N, D)\)

  • observation: tensor of shape \((T, N, C, H, W)\)

  • dt: tensor of shape \((T)\)

where:

\[\begin{split}\begin{aligned} N ={} & \text{batch size} \\ T ={} & \text{sequence length} \\ D ={} & \mathrm{dim}(\u) \\ \end{aligned}\end{split}\]
References in paper:

  • During inference, vt is computed as vt = (xt − xt−1)/∆t, with xt ∼ q(xt|It) and xt−1 ∼ q(xt−1|It−1).

  • we added an additional regularization term to the latent space, KL(q(x|I)‖N (0, 1))
